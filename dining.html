PANORAMA RESTAURANT

Restaurant has a panoramic view of the gentle hills add up to a dining experience evocative of the best in the world. The Multi-cuisine Panorama restaurant serves an exotic range of Indian, Tandoor, Continental, Chinese & traditional Kumauni cuisines. The restaurant offers elaborate buffet Dinner, breakfast and lunch as well.

RIVER SIDE RESTAURANT

Explore the whole refreshing dine experience at the banks of the river. The ambiance becomes more magical when the delicious food is accompanied by a fresh breeze and mesmerizing sounds of the rippling river. The river-side restaurant offers a one-of-a-kind fine dine where food pleases your taste buds and the atmosphere soothes your soul

CURLY CHAIRS RESTAURANT

The open grill restaurant, striving to make your experience exceptional by serving live prepared snacks. These outstanding delicacies and the high-end yet comfortable atmosphere together are ready to enhance your dining affair. CURLY CHAIRS never fails to amaze its guests with exotic food, friendly service and the culture they love to be in.
